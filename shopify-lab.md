# Shopify Lab – Step-by-Step Live Demo Flow  

## Objective
To demonstrate how an online marketplace can be built using **Shopify** and to understand the **end-to-end e-commerce workflow** including product listing, order placement, payment, and analytics.


## Prerequisites
- Internet connection  
- Email ID (for Shopify login)  
- Web browser (Chrome / Edge / Firefox)

---

## Demo Overview
In this demo, we will:
- Create a Shopify store
- Add products (cataloging)
- View the storefront
- Simulate an order
- Observe order management
- Understand payment, shipping, and analytics

---

## Step 1: Access Shopify
1. Open a web browser  
2. Go to: **https://www.shopify.com**
3. Click on **Start free trial**
4. Sign up using an email ID or Google account

**Concept Explained:**  
Independent e-commerce website vs third-party marketplace (Flipkart/Amazon)

---

## Step 2: Explore Shopify Admin Dashboard
After login, observe the **Shopify Admin Panel**.

Key sections:
- Home
- Products
- Orders
- Customers
- Analytics
- Settings

**Concept Explained:**  
The admin dashboard acts as the **control center** of an e-commerce business.

---

## Step 3: Add a Product (Cataloging)
1. Click **Products → Add product**
2. Enter:
   - Product title  
   - Product description  
   - Price  
3. Upload a sample product image  
4. Click **Save**

**Concepts Covered:**
- Product digitization  
- Cataloging  
- Pricing strategy  

---

## Step 4: View the Storefront
1. Click **Online Store** from the left menu  
2. Click **View your store**

**Observation:**  
This is the **customer-facing website**, also known as the **e-commerce marketplace interface**.

---

## Step 5: Place a Test Order
1. Open the storefront in a new tab  
2. Add a product to the cart  
3. Click **Checkout**  
4. Use Shopify’s **test payment / bogus payment option**

**Concepts Covered:**
- Order generation  
- Checkout process  
- Online transaction flow  

---

## Step 6: View Orders in Admin Panel
1. Return to Shopify Admin  
2. Click **Orders**
3. Open the newly placed order

**Concepts Covered:**
- Order receipt  
- Order tracking  
- Post-sales processing  

---

## Step 7: Payment & Shipping Settings (Overview Only)
1. Click **Settings → Payments**
   - Observe available payment gateways (UPI, Cards, Wallets)
2. Click **Shipping and Delivery**
   - Observe shipping zones and delivery methods

**Concepts Covered:**
- Electronic payment systems  
- Logistics integration  

---

## Step 8: Analytics Overview
1. Click **Analytics**
2. Observe:
   - Store visitors  
   - Orders  
   - Sales data  

**Concepts Covered:**
- Web analytics  
- Business performance tracking

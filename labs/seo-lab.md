# SEO Practical Lab – On-Page and Off-Page SEO Tools

## Objective
Learn to use SEO tools to analyze website performance and improve search rankings.

---

## Learning Outcomes
- Identify on-page and off-page SEO issues
- Use free and popular SEO tools
- Suggest improvements for better visibility

---

## Step 1: Introduction
**On-page SEO:** Meta tags, headings, URLs, images  
**Off-page SEO:** Backlinks, citations, social shares

---

## Step 2: SEO Tools
**On-page:** Google Search Console, SEOptimer, Yoast SEO  
**Off-page:** Moz Link Explorer, Ahrefs, Ubersuggest  
**Other tools:** Screaming Frog, PageSpeed Insights

---

## Step 3: Hands-On Activity
**On-page SEO:**  
1. Open your website  
2. Use SEOptimer or PageSpeed Insights  
3. Identify missing meta tags, broken links, alt text  
4. Record improvements

**Off-page SEO:**  
1. Use Moz Link Explorer or Ubersuggest  
2. Check backlinks, domain authority  
3. Compare 2 competitor websites  
4. Record insights

---

## Step 4: Case Study
- Select Flipkart / Amazon / Meesho  
- Analyze on-page & off-page SEO  
- Record what is good & improvement areas

---

## Step 5: Student Deliverables
- Screenshots of tool reports  
- Short report with findings & suggestions  
- Optional slides summarizing case study

---

## Step 6: Viva Questions
1. Difference between on-page and off-page SEO?  
2. Importance of backlinks?  
3. Impact of page speed on ranking?  
4. Which tool was most helpful and why?

---

## Note
Use free versions of tools. This lab is **for educational purposes only**.
